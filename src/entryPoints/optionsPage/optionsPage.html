Good to know

  • On browser pages (about:, chrome://, add-on stores) the shortcut can't run; use the toolbar popup to adjust the shortcut or open settings.
  • Live trails are session-only and clear when the browser closes. Named saved trails persist locally; neither is sent over the network.
  • Saved trail names and complete navigation trees must be unique. Shorter or longer versions of a path can still be saved separately.
  • Each tab keeps its most recent 100 pages; jumping back and branching off drops the abandoned forward entries, just like browser history.
